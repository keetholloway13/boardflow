// features
Everything your
team needs.
📋
Daily Task Board
Every employee gets their own column. Add, assign, and prioritize tasks across your whole team in one view.
🔒
Secure PIN Login
No emails or passwords to manage. Each employee logs in with their name and a personal PIN.
⚡
Priority Levels
Mark tasks as high, mid, or low priority. Sort columns instantly so your team always knows what to do first.
💬
Task Notes
Add notes and comments to any task. Keep context right where the work is — no separate chat needed.
📅
History & Reports
View up to 30 days of completed work per employee. Export to CSV for payroll, audits, or client reports.
👑
Multi-Admin Support
Promote trusted employees to admin. They can manage tasks and the board without sharing your company PIN.
